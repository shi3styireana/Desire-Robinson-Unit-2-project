INFORMATION ABOUT EATING DISORDERS

Did you know that 30 million people in the U.S. have eating disorders? And 95 percent of people with eating disorders are between the ages of 12 and 25. Eating disorders have the highest risk of death of any mental illness. Eating disorders can effect anyone, no matter your gener or race.

Some signs of an eating disorder could be dramatic weight loss, limited range of preferred foods that becomes narrower over time, picky eating that progressively worsens, fears of choking or vomiting. And no body image disturbance or fear of weight gain.

Many things could lead to having a eatng disorder such as low self esteem, poor body image, challenges in relationships with friends and family, abuse, trauma, striving to be perfect in one or more areas and social pressure to be thin.
